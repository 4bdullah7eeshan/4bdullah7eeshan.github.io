title: Market
---

I have stumbled across two definitions of market:

1. **Market** is *where* our customers are. Here, it means literally *where* pointing to an area/region/space/sector. For example, if our customers are in the US, we can refer to them as our US market. If our customers are in the oil & gas sector, we can refer to them as oil & gas market.

2. **Market** is any arena where business dealings happen.
