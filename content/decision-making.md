## When not to make decisions

There are many wrong parameters on which decisions shouldn't me made


### Decisions shouldn't be based on burnt out

We shouldn’t take decisions when we are [[burnout \|burnt out]] because most of the time they turn out to be wrong ones.

We must do the following:

1. Diagnose if we are going through a phase of burn out.
2. Take steps to heal.

And only then can we think clearly!
